Network Security

What Is Network Security

A network is a group of devices that are connected together either through physical wires or wirelessly. We use networks every day and you are part of a network reading this right now!

“Network security is any activity designed to protect the usability and integrity of your network and data.”

In simple terms, network security is ways of keeping you and your data safe and secure on the internet.
