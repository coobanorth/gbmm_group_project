Network Security

Mini Quiz

What Is Network Security?

A network is a group of devices that are connected together either through physical wires or wirelessly. We use networks every day and you are part of a network reading this right now!

Acording to Cisco a large network hardware manufacturer "Network security is any activity designed to protect the usability and integrity of your network and data."

In simple terms, network security is ways of keeping you and your data safe and secure on the internet.

Network Security Technologies

These technologies have been put in place in various places such as your operating system to detect and prevent attacks that can be a threat to you and your data.

  • Firewalls
  • VPN
  • Anti-Virus
  • Encryption
  • Intrusion Detection Systems

How Do I Be More Network Aware?

The Internet can be a scary place if you don’t know what to look out for. Being network aware is knowing how to look out for things that could harm you or your data. Here are some tips to help you be network aware:

  1. Checking that websites start with https
  2. Keep anti-virus and malware protection software up to date as hackers are constantly evolving
  3. Don’t download files from websites that seem too good to be true
  4. Don’t give bank card or personal information away to websites that you don’t need to

What Laws And Regulations Are There To Keep Me Safe?

Network and Information Security Regulations make sure that organisations implement effective cybersecurity measures. If not fines of up to £17 million can be given to them.

GDPR ensures that organisations keep the information they collect secure and safe.
