Network Security

Network
Multiple devices that communicate with each other
Firewall
A software designed to monitor IP traffic coming in to and out of your device
VPN
Virtual Private Network
Anti-Virus
Software that protects a device from harmfull attacks
Encryption
A process of jumbling up data so the untrained eye cannot read it
Intrusion Detection Systems
A device or software application that monitors a network or systems for malicious activity or policy violations
GDPR
General Data Protection Regulation

Social Media Security

Phishing
Pretending to be somthing you are not to gather information or get money out of people
Data Breach
Data becoming available publicly or on the black market that shouldnt be
Social Engineering
The use of deception to manipulate individuals into handing over confidential or personal information that may be used for fraudulent purposes
Brute Force Attacks
Having repeated attempts at somthing until successful
Account Recovery
Using your personal information to regain access to a hijacked account
